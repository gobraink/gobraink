GOBRA I.N.K.M. HIGH SCHOOL was established on 22 January in 1928, and it is managed by the Govt. of West Bengal, Department of School Education.

S.I.(Digha Circle)

It is located in Rural area in RAMNAGAR -I block of PURBA MEDINIPUR district of West Bengal. This school is approachable in all weather conditions.

The school consists of Grades from 5 to 10(under WBBSE) and from 11 to 12(under WBCHSE). The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school. In this school academic session starts in January.

The school has Government building. It has got 16 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 11 boys toilet and it is functional. and 5 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has also a mid-day-meal dining hall.The school has also a hostel.The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms.

The school has a library and has 1170 books in its library.

The school has 12 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school has a Computer Aided Learning Lab. and also has an ICT Lab..
